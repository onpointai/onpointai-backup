Neural Style Transfer

As a wokflow exercise I attempted to render the image of an F1 car in the style of Picasso! I used images of recent Racing Point and Renault F1 cars.

I trained it on Google Colab using both Tensorflow and Pytorch (separately ofcourse!)

The resulting images were very similar.
